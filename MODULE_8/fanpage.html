About Cracker

Cracker is an American alternative rock band led by singer David Lowery and guitarist Johnny Hickman. Very successful in both the U.S. and Europe, their fans refer to themselves as Crumbs.

An interesting mix of Country and Rock, many consider them to be enlightened, educated, worldly hillbillies. Humor, sometimes dark, sometimes whimsical is one of the things that they are best known for.

The band was formed in 1990 and in 1993 released the album that they are best known for - Kerosene Hat. It included their hit songs "Euro-Trash Girl," "Low" and "Get Off This," as well as a cover of the Grateful Dead's "Loser." The album sold almost half a million copies that year and eventually almost reached platinum status.
